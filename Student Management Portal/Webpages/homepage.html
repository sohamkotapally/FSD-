CVR College of Engineering is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education in engineering and technology. Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Information Technology, Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and more.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and recreational areas for students. We are committed to fostering a vibrant learning environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and critical thinking.

At CVR College of Engineering, we prioritize the holistic development of our students. We offer various extracurricular activities, clubs, and events to enhance their skills and provide opportunities for personal growth. Our dedicated faculty members are passionate about teaching and mentoring students to help them achieve their academic and career goals.

Join us at CVR College of Engineering and embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and success in the field of engineering!
