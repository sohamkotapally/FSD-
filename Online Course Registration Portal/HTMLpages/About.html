About Our Institute

Institute History

The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 'useful learning'. Established with a strong foundation, CVR College of Engineering has consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions, promoting innovation, research, and holistic student development.

Vision

To be a state-of-the-art institution of engineering in pursuit of excellence, creating highly competent and socially responsible technocrats who can meet global challenges and contribute significantly to society.

Mission

To produce quality engineers by providing state-of-the-art education, fostering a culture of research and innovation, and establishing strong industry-academia ties to ensure our graduates are future-ready and technically profound.
